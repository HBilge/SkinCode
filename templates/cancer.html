Skin Cancer

Skin cancer is one of the most common types of cancer. It can occur anywhere on the skin. Although melanoma is the most dangerous type, it is crucial to be aware of non-melanoma skin cancers and understand the forms they can take. The most common kinds of non-melanoma skin cancers are Basal Cell Carcinoma and Squamous Cell Carcinoma. They typically show skin lesions that show non-natural growth.

There are multiple reasons for skin cancer to be on the rise for decades. For example, increased tanning, high sun exposure, or lack of protection from the sun damages the skin. On the other hand, there are multiple treatment options, if the cancer is detected earlier.
