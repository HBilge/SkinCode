About SkinCode

SkinCode is a skin cancer detection web application which gives predictions for skin cancer by using trained neural networks. Users can check their skin regularly, and keep track of changes in their skin spots. On the other hand, it gives advice and recommendations to overcome the disease.

The technology used allows users to upload images of spots into the system. The images uploaded should be high-quality skin spots. Based on the trained neural networks, the system analyzes the image and gives predictions as cancer or not. The images that users uploaded are saved to the database so that the users can check their profile and monitor changes in their skin.

The system gives advice along with the risk assessments. It aims to better explain this cancer type and prevention ways. On the other hand, it gives recommendations for dermatologists.

The purpose of this application is to allow users to periodically check the skin and keep track of changes in the spots, as well as to educate users about skin cancer, what causes it, how it is treated, and how to avoid it.

SkinCode helps to diagnose skin cancer at an early stage when the chance of treatment is high. With trained neural networks, the system analyzes the image of a skin spot and give a prediction result along with advice on what you should do next. On the other hand, it gives recommendations for doctors.
